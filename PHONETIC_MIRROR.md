THE PHONETIC MIRROR CHART
A reciprocal substitution cipher based on mouth shape and articulation.

| Category       | Sound 1       | ↔ | Sound 2       | Transform |
|----------------|---------------|---|---------------|-----------|
| PLOSIVES       | /p/ (pin)     | ↔ | /b/ (bin)     | p ↔ b     |

|                | /k/ (coat)    | ↔ | /g/ (goat)    | k ↔ g     |
| FRICATIVES     | /f/ (fan)     | ↔ | /v/ (van)     | f ↔ v     |
|                | /θ/ (thin)    | ↔ | /ð/ (this)    | th ↔ th   |
|                | /s/ (sip)     | ↔ | /z/ (zip)     | s ↔ z     |

| NASALS/LIQUIDS | /m/ (map)     | ↔ | /n/ (nap)     | m ↔ n     |
|                | /l/ (lip)     | ↔ | /ɹ/ (rip)     | l ↔ r     |
|                | /w/ (wet)     | ↔ | /j/ (yet)     | w ↔ y     |

HOW TO USE
   
1. Convert to Sound: Ignore the spelling. Say the word out loud.
2. Swap: Find the sound in the chart and switch it.
3. Result: You now have a new word that sounds vaguely related but completely foreign.
